Lantern Guild is a cozy fantasy idle-clicker on Steam (mobile port post-launch). Visual identity is locked as "Lantern-Lit Pixel Diorama" — every scene feels like a warm miniature you want to pick up.

**Visual Identity Anchor (locked):**
- Named direction: Lantern-Lit Pixel Diorama
- One-liner: Every scene must feel like a warm miniature you want to pick up.
- Silhouette-first class design (32px readable)
- Depth via blur, not parallax (HD-2D tilt-shift DoF)
- Warm palette anchors: ambers, dusk-purples, gold highlights; no pure-saturated red/green

**Game Pillars:**
1. Respect the Player's Time
2. Every Class Feels Distinct
3. Matchup Is a Decision, Not a Reflex
4. HD-2D Pixel Pride

**Class Roster:**
- MVP (3): Warrior, Mage, Rogue
- V1.0 additions (3): Cleric, Ranger, Tactician (names subject to art-bible final call)

**5-Biome Plan (V1.0):**
- MVP: 1 biome (to be named in art bible)
- V1.0: 5 biomes (forest, ruins, cavern proposed — art bible defines all 5 with palette rules)

**Platform:**
- Primary: PC Steam + Steam Deck (1280x800)
- Post-launch: iOS/Android
- Touch-first UI from MVP (no hover, no right-click; ≥44px tap targets)
- Engine: Godot 4.6 + GDScript

**Named References:**
- Octopath Traveler (HD-2D visual north star)
- Silksong (pixel-art atmosphere precision)
- Devil Lord: Half of World (idle loop genre reference)
- Studio Ghibli interiors (warm lamplight palette)
- Tabletop miniature photography (shallow DoF diorama framing)
- Pentiment (illuminated-manuscript UI accents)

**Art Bible drafted:** 2026-04-18 — Sections 1-7 and 9 delivered as draft content for assembly into assets/art/art-bible.md

**Why:** Art bible governs 6-12 months of asset work; locked visual identity anchor is the editorial baseline.
**How to apply:** Any new asset category, biome, or class proposal must be checked against the locked visual identity anchor and the pillar list before proceeding.
