## Overview

`BiomeDungeonDatabase` is a typed accessor over three nested resource types:
`Biome`, `Dungeon`, `Floor` (each `extends GameData`). Floors hold deterministic
`enemy_list: Array[Dictionary]{enemy_id, count}` — no RNG, no probabilistic
spawns. Schema validation cascades invalid floors → dungeons → biomes
(UNAVAILABLE state) without dropping the rest of the catalog.

`planned_v1` biomes load empty-dungeons-OK and are filtered out by Floor/Biome
Unlock from the assignment UI. Forest Reach (MVP) is exactly 1 dungeon × 5
floors with `floor_index {1..5}` gap-free, one boss floor (F5, Ancient
Rootking).

validation (Floor.enemy_id → EnemyDatabase resolution) at boot is the trickiest
part — order-of-load matters and is locked by the rank table.
